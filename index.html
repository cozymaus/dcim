About Me

Hello! My name is Lys. I'm a Cuban-American aspiring front end developer and indie game developer graduating soon with a BA in Information Technology and Informatics and a minor in DCIM from Rutgers University in New Brunswick, NJ. I am a detail-oriented person who values transparency, frankness, resourcefulness, and simplicity. I may be introverted and soft-spoken, but I have passionate feelings about many things and always rise to a challenge. I love animals (particularly the small ones), and when not blogging or coding I'm probably drawing for fun, watching anime, dancing, playing computer games, reading about true crime, or coming up with new story ideas. I speak fluent Spanish and English and some French and German. I run a blog and YouTube channel about art, programming, and working on the Web. I have plans for a WordPress theme store and a game I'm working on right now. You can see my projects on Github!
